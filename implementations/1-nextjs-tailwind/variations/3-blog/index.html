Implementing AI in Enterprise: Lessons from Microsoft Copilot Rollout

How we launched AI Week with 12 strategic partners and successfully rolled out Microsoft Copilot across Jemena's corporate functions, optimizing business processes and workflows.

The Challenge

When tasked with implementing AI capabilities across Jemena's corporate functions, I knew this wasn't just about technology deployment—it was about cultural transformation and change management at scale.

Strategic Approach

Our approach was methodical and focused on demonstrating value quickly:

  • Partnership Strategy - Engaged 12 technology strategic partners including Microsoft, AWS, and ServiceNow
  • Education First - Launched AI Week to educate teams and showcase possibilities
  • Use Case Development - Identified specific business process optimization opportunities
  • Phased Rollout - Implemented Microsoft Copilot in stages with feedback loops

Implementation Results

The results exceeded expectations across multiple dimensions:

Key Outcomes:

• 40% reduction in document processing time
• 60% improvement in meeting efficiency
• 85% user adoption rate within first month
• $2.3M estimated annual productivity savings

Technology Integration
